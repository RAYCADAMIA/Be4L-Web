# Project Details: Be4L

**Be4L** (pronounced "Be For Life") is a social platform designed to encourage real-world interaction and minimize screen time.

## Mission
> **"Minimize screen time, maximize life time."**
> **"Always For Life"**

The goal is to provide a platform that facilitates real-life activities, adventures, and connections without keeping users glued to their screens.

## Core Features

### 1. Social Feed & Dual Camera
- **Dual Camera Capture**: Users capture the moment using both front and back cameras simultaneously.
- **Real-time Sharing**: Share authentic moments with friends.
- **Contextual Metadata**: Posts include location, music tracks, and tagged friends.

### 2. Quests
- **Side Quests**: Casual activities like "Sunset Bouldering" or "Midnight Gallery Hop".
- **Competitions**: Structured events with potential prizes (e.g., "Pickleball Tournament").
- **Host & Join**: Users can host their own quests or join others.
- **Categories**: Adventure, Sports, Social, etc.

### 3. Booking
- **Venues & Events**: Integrated booking for courts, venues, tickets, and more.
- **Seamless Experience**: Browse and book directly within the app.

### 4. Interactive Profile
- **Identity**: Custom usernames ("Name4L"), bios, and avatars.
- **Streaks**: Gamified engagement tracking (e.g., "Streak Count").

### 5. Chat & Connection
- **Direct Messaging**: Connect with friends and coordinate plans.
- **Quest Invites**: Send and receive invites to quests directly in chat.
